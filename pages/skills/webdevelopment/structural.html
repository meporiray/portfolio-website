A. Structural & Semantic Tags

These define the "skeleton" of your page.

  • <html>: The root element.
  • <head>: Contains metadata (not visible to users).
  • <body>: Contains all visible content.
  • <header>: The top section (logo, nav).
  • <nav>: Navigation links.
  • <main>: The primary content area.
  • <section>: A thematic grouping of content.
  • <article>: Self-contained, reusable content.
  • <aside>: Sidebar content.
  • <footer>: The bottom section.
